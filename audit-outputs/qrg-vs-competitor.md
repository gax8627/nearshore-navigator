# Section 4: QRG Competitor Comparison

## Competitor: Tetakawi (`tetakawi.com`) vs Nearshore Navigator

| Dimension | Tetakawi Score | Nearshore Navigator Score | Gap / Advantage |
|-----------|----------------|---------------------------|-----------------|
| **Expertise** | 9/10 | 9/10 | Tie. Both show deep manufacturing knowledge. |
| **Authoritativeness** | 9/10 | 7.5/10 | **Tetakawi Win.** They have decades of legacy backlinks and established brand search volume. |
| **Trustworthiness** | 8/10 | 8.5/10 | **Nearshore Navigator Win.** Tetakawi's site feels slightly dated; Nearshore Navigator's new Enterprise Gateway design and modern UX feels more transparent and secure to a modern 2026 executive. |
| **Needs Met** | 7/10 | 9/10 | **Nearshore Navigator Win.** Tetakawi buries cost metrics behind long lead forms. Nearshore Navigator offers immediate clarity with direct cost calculator CTAs. |
| **Main Content Quality**| 8.5/10 | 8/10 | **Tetakawi Win (Slightly).** Their sheer volume of blog content (hundreds of posts) currently outpaces Nearshore Navigator's tighter Pillar Page strategy. |

### Strategic Takeaway:
Nearshore Navigator wins on User Experience (UX), Trust, and Needs Met. The remaining gap is purely historical authority and content volume, which will close as the China-Plus-One sequence scales.
