# Section 6: Competitor Gap Analysis

## Competitors Analyzed:
1. `tetakawi.com` (DA: 62)
2. `ivemsa.com` (DA: 45)
3. `tacna.net` (DA: 41)

## Off-Page & Authority Gaps
- **Backlink Velocity**: Top competitors are acquiring 10-15 high-quality backlinks per month primarily through industry reports and press releases. Nearshore Navigator is at 0 velocity.
- **Directory Presence**: Missing from `ThomasNet`, `IndustryNet`, and specific municipal economic development pages in Baja California where Tetakawi is listed.
- **Knowledge Panel**: Both Tetakawi and IVEMSA trigger branded Knowledge Panels; Nearshore Navigator does not.

## On-Page & Content Gaps
- **Content Volume**: Tetakawi has over 300+ blog articles answering highly specific long-tail queries. While our Pillar Page strategy is more efficient, we lack the sheer topical footprint.
- **Case Studies / Proof**: Competitors feature video testimonials and 10+ PDF case studies. We currently lack a dedicated "Success Stories" section.

## Priority Strategic Recommendations:
1. **Accelerate Phase 3 & 4**: We must execute the pending directory submissions (ProMexico, Site Selection) and deploy the HARO templates immediately to build the required base Domain Authority to cross the DA 30 threshold.
2. **Launch "Success Stories" Hub**: Even if anonymized, B2B case studies are critical for both E-E-A-T trust and long-tail lead generation.
