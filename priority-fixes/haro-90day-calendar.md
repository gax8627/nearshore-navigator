# 90-Day HARO Execution Calendar

## Goal: 3+ Published Placements (DA ≥ 50) in 90 Days

### DAILY RHYTHM (Monday - Friday)
- **5:45 AM ET (Optional / For Early Staff)** — Check HARO Morning Email, flag relevant queries.
- **12:45 PM ET** — Check HARO Midday Email, respond to any flagged queries.
- **5:45 PM ET** — Check HARO Evening Email, respond to any flagged queries.
*(Golden Rule: Respond to queries where ≥ 2 templates apply. Always personalize to the exact query context before sending).*

### WEEKLY RHYTHM
- **Every Monday Morning**: Log into Featured.com and Qwoted to scan for non-email queries.
- **Every Friday Afternoon**: Audit the `/priority-fixes/journalist-tracker.csv` file. Update statuses, track inbound responses.
