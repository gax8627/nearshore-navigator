# SEO Consistency System: Weekly Workflows

## The Weekly Checklist

*   **MONDAY**: Publish or schedule 1 high-authority LinkedIn Article or Post (e.g., "Total Landed Cost Breakdown 2026").
*   **TUESDAY**: Review HARO, Qwoted, and Terkel for journalist queries regarding supply chain, manufacturing, or nearshoring. Pitch 1-2 relevant responses.
*   **WEDNESDAY**: Respond to 2 genuine questions on r/supplychain, r/manufacturing, or LinkedIn Groups using the established expert templates.
*   **THURSDAY**: Follow up on any pending directory submissions (Index Maquiladoras, Crunchbase, etc.).
*   **FRIDAY**: Audit the `backlink-tracker.csv`. Log any new inbound leads and attribute them to the correct source ("Client mentioned finding us on Clutch/Area Development").
*   **MONTHLY**: Request 1 new verified review on Clutch or G2 from a recently onboarded advisory client.
