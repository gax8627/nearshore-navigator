## 2. Location Page Audit Directives
When generating or updating `/locations/[city]` pages, ensure they:
*   Have a unique intro paragraph solving specific intent (e.g., "Tijuana for medical devices" vs. "Hermosillo for automotive").
*   Name local industrial parks (do not use generic "we have great parks here" text).
*   Provide local workforce stats and exact drive times to US ports of entry (e.g., "180 miles to Nogales").
